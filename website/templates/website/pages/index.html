EcoSocial Dynamics International (ESDI) is a USA-based private consultancy company that advises governments, international organizations, and private corporations in the Horn of Africa (HoA) on how to improve the livelihood of the peoples of the region. Based on hard data, the region has many favorable eco-social systems that can comfortably sustain the population. However, despite its natural endowments, the HoA is one of the most inhospitable regions in the world.

The Horn of Africa is a tapestry of ecology and cultures that have traditionally complemented one another. In recent years new faulty lines have appeared in the tapestry—i.e., worsening state-society relations, dwindling resources, tribal wars, terrorism, and mass displacement of peoples. Left unaddressed for so many years, these have severely reduced the habitability of the HoA. Mass migration and internal displacement of peoples are common occurrences.

What we do!

ESDI monitors and researches on the drivers of eco-social challenges:

  • Climate Change
  • Weak governance systems
  • Over exploitation of the commons
  • Social strife
  • Recurrent famine
